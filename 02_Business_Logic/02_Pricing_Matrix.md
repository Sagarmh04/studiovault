Pricing Matrix (Phase 1 Locked)

StudioVault pricing is structured around two operational units:

1. Branch Plans (Day-to-day studio execution)
2. Organization Plans (Multi-branch governance + HQ analytics)

Billing Model:
- StudioVault does not use an internal wallet system.
- All usage-based charges are added to the month-end invoice.
- Payment is collected monthly with enforcement rules.

---

1. Branch Subscription Plans (India Baseline)

A) Free Branch Plan ("Starter")
- Price: ₹0/month
- Active Projects Cap: Max 3 concurrent active projects
- Storage Included: 2GB (Proxy-only via Smart Select)
- Branding: "Powered by StudioVault" badge required
- Online Booking: Enabled
- Platform Processing Fee: 5% total per transaction
  - Includes Payment Gateway (~2.5%) + StudioVault Platform Fee (~2.5%)

Best For:
- Hobbyists and single-studio trial adoption

---

B) Pro Branch Plan ("Studio")
- Price: ₹299/month (per branch)
- Active Projects: Unlimited
- Storage Included: 50GB (RAW supported)
- Branding: White-label (badge removed)
- Analytics: Enhanced branch-level reporting
- Platform Processing Fee: 3.5% total per transaction
  - Includes Payment Gateway (~2.5%) + StudioVault Platform Fee (~1.0%)

Best For:
- Professional studios running frequent paid bookings

---

C) Agency Branch Mode ("Enterprise Execution Tier")
- Price: ₹1999/month (per branch)
- Includes all Pro features plus:
  - Vendor Portal Access (Phase 2)
  - Advanced Production Workflow (Video Ops)
  - Audit & Approval Governance Hooks
  - Priority support + SLA options

Platform Processing Fee:
- Enterprise execution tier does not charge StudioVault fee
- Only payment gateway fees apply (~2.5%)

Best For:
- High-volume premium branches inside larger agencies

---

2. Organization Plans (Multi-Branch Governance)

Organization Mode activates when:
- Org has more than 1 Branch

Org Plans sit above branch subscriptions.

---

A) Free Organization Plan
- Price: ₹0/month HQ fee
- Governance: Minimal
- Global Analytics: Limited
- Branch Billing: Branch subscriptions apply normally

Example:
- 3 Branches × ₹299 + ₹0 HQ Fee

---

B) Paid Organization Plan ("HQ Mode")
- Price: ₹999/month (Org-level governance fee)
- Unlocks:
  - Global Command Center analytics
  - Cross-branch audit visibility
  - Approval thresholds (refunds/discounts)
  - Package Library governance
  - Advanced agency controls

Example:
- 3 Branches × ₹299 + ₹999 HQ Fee

---

C) Bundled Organization Plans (Future Scope)
StudioVault may offer bundles such as:

- ₹2499/month = Org HQ Mode + 2 Agency Branches included

Note:
- Bundle pricing is value-calibrated and finalized post-launch.

4. Billing Enforcement & Non-Payment Policy

Billing Cycle:
- Monthly invoice generated at month-end

Grace Period:
- 7 days after invoice due date

After Grace Period:
1. Uploads blocked
2. Delivery Portal frozen (clients see "Gallery Temporarily Unavailable")

Data Retention Enforcement:
- Day 45: Final Warning issued + One-click Export ZIP offered
- Day 60: Storage eligible for deletion if unpaid

This policy is explicitly defined in Terms and Compliance documentation.
