In-App Banners & Forced Update Policy

StudioVault requires operational communication inside the app.

---

## Banner Types
- System outages (SEV1)
- Billing overdue warnings
- New feature announcements

---

## Forced Update Rule (Desktop)

Desktop apps may require forced updates when:

- Security fixes are critical
- Sync protocol changes
- Compliance enforcement changes

Behavior:
- User cannot proceed until updated
- Emergency patches bypass optional delay
