Global Constants & Limits

## 1. Storage & Media

**Free Plan Limit:** 2GB (Strict enforcement)
- Free users cannot upload RAW files.
- Uploads must occur through the "Smart Select" Desktop Bridge (proxy-only).

**Paid Plan Limit:** 50GB (RAW allowed)

**Overage Rate:**
- Charged per GB/month (Region-specific, e.g., ₹4.5/GB in India)

**Data Retention:**
- Active Projects: Immediate access (Hot Storage - R2)
- Cancelled/Unpaid Accounts: Subject to billing enforcement and deletion timelines

---

## 2. Communications (SMS/WhatsApp)

**Included Quota:**
- 60 notifications per month (covers ~15 bookings)

**Overage Handling:**
- Pay-per-use charges are added to the month-end invoice
- No wallet deduction occurs

If an account is overdue beyond the grace period:
- High-priority notifications may be paused
- Email remains as the fallback channel

---

## 3. Platform Identity

**Free Tier Branding:**
- "Powered by StudioVault" mandatory in footer

**Subdomain Logic:**
- [brand].studiovault.in (One URL per Organization)

**Branch Selector Behavior:**
- If Org has 1 Branch → Direct landing
- If Org has >1 Branch → Visitor selects Location/Branch
